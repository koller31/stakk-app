Delete Your Data

Stakk - Digital ID Wallet  |  Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Important: Stakk stores all data locally on your device. No data is stored on any server. Deleting your data is instant and permanent.

How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. Choose any of the following methods:

Delete Individual Cards

  1. Open the Stakk app
  2. Navigate to the card you want to delete
  3. Tap the card to open it
  4. Tap the delete icon and confirm
  5. The card image is securely overwritten and permanently removed

Delete All Data (Full Reset)

  1. Open the Stakk app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Clear All Data
  4. Confirm the action
  5. All cards, images, preferences, and authentication data are permanently deleted

Uninstall the App

  1. Uninstall Stakk from your device
  2. All app data, encrypted images, and preferences are automatically removed by the operating system

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your data, the following is permanently removed:

  1. All scanned card images (securely overwritten before deletion)
  2. All card metadata (names, notes, categories)
  3. All OCR-extracted text
  4. Your PIN and authentication settings
  5. Theme and display preferences
  6. Any business badge connections and tokens

Data Recovery

Because Stakk does not use cloud storage or backups, deleted data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to delete your data before proceeding.
